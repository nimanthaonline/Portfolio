Tech Behind the Curtain Video Series

Tech video reels series for Fcode Labs

A video series that highlights the technical expertise of Fcode Labs' team by sharing educational and engaging insights on software engineering and technology through social media platforms.

I led the production and post-production process, including videography, editing, and motion graphics design to deliver engaging and informative content.

Scope of Work:

  • Videography & Lighting: Planned and executed all on-set camera work and lighting design to achieve a professional visual aesthetic aligned with the brand identity.
  • Editing: Edited raw footage into polished episodes, incorporating pacing, narrative clarity, and visual consistency across the series.
  • Audio Processing: Enhanced voice clarity, removed background noise, and balanced audio levels for optimal listening experience across various devices.
  • Motion Graphics & Animations: Improved audio quality, including voice, music, and effects.
  • AI Voiceovers: Designed and integrated motion graphics, lower thirds, animated transitions, and branding elements to enhance viewer engagement and support key messaging.

Project information

  • Category Videography, Editing & Motion Graphics
  • Tools used Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, After Effects, Adobe Premiere Pro, Element 3D
