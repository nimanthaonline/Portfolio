Playpointz

Playpointz App – Social Media Ad Videos

Playpointz is a Sri Lankan Play-to-Win mobile gaming app developed by AVANUX, offering users the chance to earn rewards by engaging in fun, interactive games.

To promote the app to a new generation of gamers, a series of animated videos was created for social media. The goal was to deliver high-energy, visually engaging content that would resonate with young audiences and clearly communicate the app’s features and benefits in a matter of seconds.

Project information
