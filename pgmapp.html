Progress Magic Coaching App

Progress Magic AI Coaching app release video

ProgressMagic is a professional coaching app designed to empower coaches and consultants to build, manage, and grow their practices.

A dynamic promotional video for ProgressMagic, a coaching app designed to support professionals in building and managing a successful practice.

The video integrated with 2D UI animation, graphics, and sound design to effectively showcase the app’s core features and benefits.

Scope of Work:

  • UI Animations (Figma to After Effects): Translated static UI designs from Figma into polished, animated mockups in After Effects, showcasing app workflows and user interactions with smooth transitions and responsive elements.
  • Motion Graphics: Designed and animated custom graphics to enhance storytelling, highlight key app features, and maintain visual consistency throughout the video.
  • Compositing: Seamlessly integrated stock footage with layered motion graphics, UI animations, and on-screen elements, ensuring a cohesive and professional aesthetic.
  • Editing & Sound Design:Improved audio quality, including voice, music, and effects.
